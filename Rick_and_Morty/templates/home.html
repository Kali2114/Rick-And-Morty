Created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon, the show premiered on Adult Swim in 2013. It follows the interdimensional adventures of Rick Sanchez, a cynical, alcoholic genius scientist, and his good-hearted but fretful grandson Morty Smith. Together, they embark on wild and often perilous journeys across the multiverse, encountering a myriad of bizarre planets, creatures, and situations. The series is renowned for its originality, complex storytelling, and the way it balances dark humor with moments of genuine emotion. Rick, with his nihilistic worldview and penchant for creating gadgets and substances with ethically questionable uses, is contrasted with Morty, whose naivety and moral compass often provide the grounding for their escapades. The show also explores the dynamics of the Smith family, including Morty's insecure father Jerry, his dissatisfied mother Beth, who is Rick's daughter, and his assertive sister Summer. "Rick and Morty" delves into themes of existentialism, the nature of reality, and the human condition, all while parodying tropes from science fiction and pop culture. Its ability to weave high-concept scientific and philosophical ideas with slapstick comedy and satire has earned it a passionate fan base and critical acclaim.
